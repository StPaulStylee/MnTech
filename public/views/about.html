About MN Tech Co Op

We're a small tech cooperative serving nonprofits and small businesses.
Our members are Minnesota freelancers that want to provide high quality work while offering these great institutions of Minnesota a leg up on cost.

Core Values

  • We value tech!

    At the MN Tech Co op we emphasize using technology to make our clients lives easier. We embrace new tech and look for ways to integreate it into our services while also utilizing the tried and true technolgies that stand the test of time.

  • We suppport small business and non profits!

    In MN our small business and non profits often get priced out of accessing technology that will help them sustain and grow. With our highly competive pricing model, we are working to change that.

  • We embrace the cooperative model!

    We want all members of MN Tech Co op to make a profits doing what they love, while they do good in the world. While we are legally an LLC, we embrace the cooperative model - membership, community, trust, and accountability.
